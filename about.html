Our Story

Founded in 2012, GlobalSource began with a simple mission: to make sourcing from China accessible, transparent, and reliable for European businesses.

What started as a small team in Berlin has grown into a dynamic organization with offices in both Germany and China, serving hundreds of satisfied clients across Europe.

Today, we combine German precision with Chinese manufacturing expertise to deliver exceptional value to our clients.
